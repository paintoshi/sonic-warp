Sonic Warp


Transactions stream live from the Sonic network via public RPC and are rendered as stars rushing past you in a hyperspace tunnel
Star color corresponds to the transaction amount:
White: 0 S
Red: 0 - 1 S
Purple: 1 - 1,000 S
Blue: 1,000 - 100,000 S
Green: > 100,000 S
